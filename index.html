What is Bitwise:

Bitwise is a card game where players complete rows or columns on a grid with Bit Cards (0s and 1s) to form 4-bit sequences matching their target numbers for points.

Players draw and play Bit Cards strategically while Special Cards introduce unique actions. Balancing targets and disrupting opponents creates a dynamic and competitive experience.

The player with the most points wins each round, earned by completing targets and using strategic Bit and Special Cards.

Bitwise is designed for 2 to 4 players, accessible to all from casual gamers to seasoned strategists combining binary precision with card-based strategy for an engaging experience.

Story:

The story behind Bitwise is one of a passionate journey that began during the creator's time as an Informatics student in university. With a deep appreciation for both technology and games, the idea for Bitwise emerged at the intersection of these interests.

It started as a creative endeavor and a personal project, combining the fascination for binary code (0s and 1s) with the strategic depth of card games. Over the years, the concept evolved and was refined, turning into a fully-fledged card game that seamlessly blends the worlds of technology and strategy.

Bitwise is a testament to the creator's dedication to bridging these two passions and sharing the excitement with others. The game's journey continued through playtesting, development, and now, a promotion campaign, where it's introduced to a wider audience. The story of Bitwise is one of creativity, innovation, and a love for both technology and gaming, resulting in a unique and engaging card game experience.
