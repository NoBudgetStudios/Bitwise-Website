Gameplay:

Bitwise is an exciting card game where players complete rows or columns on a grid with Bit Cards (0s and 1s) to form 4-bit sequences matching target numbers for points. Players draw and play Bit Cards strategically while Special Cards introduce unique actions. Balancing targets and disrupting opponents creates a dynamic and competitive experience.

The player with the most points wins each round, earned by completing targets and using strategic Bit and Special Cards. Bitwise is designed for 2 to 4 players, accessible to all from casual gamers to seasoned strategists. It combines binary precision with card-based strategy for an engaging experience.
